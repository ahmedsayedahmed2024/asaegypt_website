About ASA

ASA - Ahmed Sayed Ahmed Chartered Accountants and Consultants Audit, tax and advisory services firm was established in May 2022, to work in the field of auditing, accounting, consulting and taxation. The firm has become a prominent place thanks to the hard work and the efforts of the professional and specialized team, in order to maintain the leadership and insist on providing excellent services to our clients in the field of accounting, auditing and consulting.
